Alzheimer's Disease (AD), accounting for 80% of dementia cases, is a neurodegenerative disease characterized by progressive loss of memory and cognition, among other neurobehavioral symptoms. Early detection and understanding of disease progression pathways are critical for effective intervention.

## Objectives

1. **Phenotyping Tools** -- Develop phenotyping tools to create a research-grade AD real-world data (RWD) cohort and novel graph neural network (GNN) techniques to identify AD progression pathways
2. **Retinal Imaging Biomarkers** -- Develop novel retinal imaging biomarkers of AD and assess the longitudinal association of retinal vasculature changes with AD disease progression
